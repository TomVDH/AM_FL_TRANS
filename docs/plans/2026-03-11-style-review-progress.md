## In Progress: Slow Ass / Slome Ezel (156 lines)

### Discrepancy Analysis Complete — Awaiting Rulings

**Lines:** 156 across E1, E2, E3, E4, E5, E6
**Bio:** Well-intentioned. Slow moving due to having arthritis. Timid, weary, unconfident. Looks up to Trusty.

### Discrepancies Found:

#### 1. CRITICAL: Stutter system broken in Dutch
In English, stutters only on B and P sounds — his signature. In Dutch, 98 non-B/P stutters across every consonant (z, m, d, h, k, w, g, n, s, r, a, l, v, f, j, o). Makes him sound like a general speech impediment rather than a specific B/P stutter.

**Ruling needed:** B/P only (matching English), broader set, or any letter?

#### 2. MAJOR: Pronouns — consistently je/jij, zero ge/gij
Only donkey so far with zero ge/gij across all episodes. Existing codex says "no ge/gij." Given project direction that most donkeys use ge/gij — is Slow an exception?

**Ruling needed:** ge/gij (Herd standard) or je/jij (character exception for timidity)?

#### 3. MAJOR: Trusty naming inconsistent
- E1, E2, E4: "Trouwe" (correct)
- E2, E4: "Trouwe Zak" (old naming, should be "Trouwe Ezel")
- E3: "Trusty" UNTRANSLATED (2x) — clear error
- E2: "Tot ziens (Trusty Ass)..." — translator note never cleaned up

**Ruling needed:** "Trouwe Ezel" always?

#### 4. MODERATE: "Oh b-b-boy" — three different Dutch renderings
- "Och j-j-jongens" / "Ach j-j-jongens" / "O j-j-jongens" / "Oh b-b-boy" (untranslated)

**Ruling needed:** Pick one fixed Dutch form?

#### 5. MODERATE: Flemish density near zero
Only 2x "nie" across 156 lines. No 't contractions, no "da," no "Nonkel," no Flemish exclamations. Most ABN-sounding donkey.

**Ruling needed:** Light/near-zero (character choice) or bring up to moderate?
